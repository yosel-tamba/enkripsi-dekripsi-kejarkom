This website provides tools for encrypting and decrypting text, enabling users to secure their messages. Additionally, we offer explanations and examples of encryption methods such as cryptography and steganography.

What is Encryption-Decryption?

Encryption is the process of converting readable data (plaintext) into an unreadable format (ciphertext) to protect it from unauthorized access. Decryption is the reverse process, converting ciphertext back to plaintext.

What is Cryptography?

Cryptography is the study and practice of secure communication techniques. It involves creating algorithms and protocols to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and authenticity of data. Cryptography is widely used in fields such as cybersecurity and digital communication.

What is Steganography?

Steganography is the art of hiding information within other files or media, such as images, audio, or video. Unlike cryptography, which focuses on securing the content, steganography focuses on concealing the existence of the message itself.
